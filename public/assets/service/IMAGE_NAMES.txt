SERVICE IMAGES - Required Filenames
=====================================

Place your service images in this directory with these exact filenames:

1. structural-steel.jpg      → Structural Steel Detailing
2. metalwork.jpg             → Metalwork Detailing
3. connections.jpg           → Connection Design
4. estimation.jpg            → Material Take-Off & Estimation
5. pointcloud.jpg            → Point Cloud to BIM Conversion
6. shop-drawings.jpg         → Shop Drawing & CNC File Generation

Image Specs:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 1200px - 1600px width
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
- File size: Under 500KB (optimized)

These images will be displayed prominently on the Service Cards
with hover effects, gradient overlays, and premium styling.
